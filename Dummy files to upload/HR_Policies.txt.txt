Company Name: ABC Corporation
Department: Human Resources
Document: Employee Leave and Benefits Policy
Version: 3.0 | Updated: March 2024

1. Annual Leave
- All full-time employees are entitled to 25 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
- Leave accrual is monthly and unused leave can be carried forward up to 10 days.

2. Sick Leave
- Employees receive 12 days of paid sick leave annually.
- A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 2 days.

3. Maternity & Paternity Leave
- Maternity leave: 6 months (paid)
- Paternity leave: 2 weeks (paid)

4. Insurance Benefits
- Health insurance is covered 100% for employees and 50% for dependents.
- Employees can opt-in for dental and vision plans.

5. Remote Work Policy
- Employees can work remotely up to 2 days a week upon manager approval.

Contact: hr@company.com for more details.
