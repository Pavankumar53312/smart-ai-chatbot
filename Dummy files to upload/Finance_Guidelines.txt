Company Name: ABC Corporation
Department: Finance
Document: Financial Guidelines & Reimbursement Policy
Version: 1.4 | Updated: February 2024

1. Travel Reimbursement
- Submit claims within 7 days of travel.
- Requires travel approval form + receipts.

2. Budget Approvals
- Department heads must pre-approve budgets exceeding $10,000.
- Submit proposals via finance-portal.abc.com

3. Payroll Schedule
- Salary is credited on the 28th of each month.
- Payslips available on the employee portal.

4. Expense Categories
- Meals: up to $50/day
- Hotel: up to $150/night
- Internet: reimbursed for remote workers (up to $40/month)

Contact: finance@company.com for questions.
